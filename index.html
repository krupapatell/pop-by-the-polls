Pop by the Polls

This is a short research article for the 2020 election. It covers four issues currently being discussed, a candidate running for President of the United States, a polling place, and a sample ballot.

Read More

Issues

These are the issues that voters and candidates are discussing this primary season.

Gay Marriage

61% of people support the legalization of gay marriage.

LGBTQ Adoption Rights

81% of people believe gay couples should have the same adoption rights as straight couples.

Abortion

62% of people are pro-choice, while 38% are pro-life.

Gun Control

50% of people believe there should be more restrictions on the current process of purchasing a gun.

Candidate

This is a person that has been nominated as a general election candidate for President fo the United States.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden is the Democratic candidate for the 2020 Presidential Election. He served as the 47th vice president of the United States. He also was a United States Senator for Delaware until 2009. Joe Biden's vision is to "build back better than ever".
